Jeffrey Chad Shiau

Demystifying Company Culture

Jeffrey Shiau helps companies design and build stronger team and customer communities. He spent the last 5 years with Impact Hub, home to over 80,000 extended start-ups and corporations. He worked with impact focused teams across the world, while observing and learning a catalog of real-world company culture practices.

He held pivotal roles with the founding management team of Mission Hub (a federate of Impact Hub Global), including the Co-Founding Catalyst and Interim Managing Director of Philadelphia, the Storytelling Co-Lead with San Francisco, and the Director of Berkeley. He also directed programming for Hub Cities, leading collaborative efforts across 15 Impact Hubs across Canada, US, and Mexico. His story on evolving into a culture and community architect can be read here.

Jeffrey studied Economics and Psychology at the University of California, San Diego, with a specific interest in behavorial economics and social psychology.
